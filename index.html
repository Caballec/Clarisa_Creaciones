My Creations

Hola! I’m Clarisa, and I create memorable magnets! Turn your favorite photos into keepsakes or unique gifts for loved ones — my magnets are designed to bring a little joy to every space. They’re also perfect for business cards, save-the-dates, and so much more. Currently, I offer square, rectangular (protrait and landscape), and circular magnets to match your style!

As an innovator, I’m always exploring new ways to bring creativity to life — from stickers to fully customized items — ensuring every product is as unique as the person receiving it. Reach out for customizable creations and sticker designs made just for you!

About Me

My work is inspired by my Oaxacan roots, which I proudly incorporate into my brand identity. For my logo, I blended my favorite animal with the vibrant tradition of alebrijes — creating a unique elephant alebrije that reflects both culture and creativity.
